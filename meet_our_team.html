Daniel and Rebecca Meyer

Co-Pastor

 Daniel Meyer is a compassionate and dedicated spiritual leader called to serve the Lord Jesus Christ. Daniel Meyer has been an integral part of our church family for years, serving as a preacher, worship leader, and teacher, with a heart for the community and a profound commitment to spreading the message of love and faith.
 Daniel Meyer brings knowledge and a deep understanding of the Scriptures to his ministry. His sermons are inspirational and practical, providing guidance for navigating the complexities of modern life through the lens of faith. Under his acts of service, our church has thrived, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
 Daniel is known for his warm and unique demeanor, always ready to serve, help, and encourage. His passion for community outreach has led us to implement programs that aim to impact the lives of those in need positively. Daniel exemplifies the teachings of love and service.

 Rebeca Meyer is an equally integral pillar of support within our church community. Her compassionate, kind, and nurturing spirit complements Daniel's ministry.
 Rebeca Meyer is vital in creating a functioning atmosphere within our church family. With a heart for the Lord, she ensures that everyone who walks through the church doors feels seen and valued.
 In addition to her role as a preacher's wife, Rebecca is a mental health professional. Her compassion and genuine care for others inspire a culture of love and acceptance within our congregation.
 Together, Daniel and Rebeca work towards representing the values of faith, love, and community that define our church family. Their partnership in ministry serves as a guiding light, steering our congregation toward spiritual growth and a deeper connection with the divine.
